Things I'm thinking about. Things I wish more people were thinking about. Written for the people building the systems everyone else will eventually depend on.

Featured · Published on LinkedIn

The EU AI Act deadline is 4 months away. Here's the thing nobody in AI governance is saying: we've already solved this problem.

Risk classification? That's customer risk tiering. Documentation requirements? That's regulatory audit readiness. Human oversight of automated decisions? That's the daily tension between automation and manual review in KYC. The AI governance conversation is dominated by technologists and lawyers. What's missing is the operational layer — the people who know how to take regulation and turn it into working processes at scale. That's not a new discipline.
