About

Two worlds.
One thread.

— · —

I'm Asaf Peled — a product operations leader who has spent over a decade inside global fintech, building the invisible scaffolding that makes companies actually work. From governance frameworks to cross-functional delivery to regulatory accountability, I've learned that the difference between a good organization and a great one is almost always operational clarity.

At the same time, I believe the most enduring investments we can make are in people — especially young people. That conviction led me to build a life skills coaching practice for children and youth, applying the same product operations thinking — structured outcomes, measurable progress, iterative design — to help the next generation develop the emotional, social, and cognitive tools they'll carry for life.

Whether I'm sitting with a leadership team untangling a broken process or working with a group of teenagers learning to navigate conflict, the underlying work is the same: helping people move from confusion to capability.
